# Kodai Construction — Website Plan

## Brand & Design Direction

- **Brand colors**: Deep Navy Blue (#1a1a6e) + Golden Yellow (#f5c518) + White
- **Style**: Modern, bold, premium — inspired by Quorum Group's cinematic feel but with Kodai's own identity
- **Interactions**: Glowing buttons, hover animations, parallax scroll effects, animated counters, and smooth page transitions
- **Loading screen**: Full-screen Kodai logo animation that fades into the homepage (like Quorum's intro)

---

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Loading / Splash Screen

- Full-screen navy background with the Kodai logo animating in (scale + glow effect)
- Transitions smoothly into the homepage after ~2 seconds

### 2. Home Page

- **Sticky navigation bar** with logo, page links (Home, Services, Portfolio, About), and a glowing "Contact" CTA button
- **Hero section**: Full-screen background image with bold headline *"High-Rise & Mid-Rise Construction Experts"* and sub-headline *"Committed to a Promise of Excellence"*, animated text reveal on load
- **Overview section**: Company intro with the Kodai Promise (3-year support, 10-year leakage guarantee, 30-year structural guarantee) displayed as animated counter cards
- **Services summary**: 3 service cards (Design & Engineering, Construction Delivery, Technical Consulting) with hover-lift effects and glowing borders, linking to the full Services page
- **Featured projects carousel**: Interactive slider showcasing Zekko Hotel, Malibu Hills, Tinuola Tower, and Atrium Homes with image overlays and "View Project" buttons
- **CTA banner**: Bold call-to-action — *"Let's Build Something That Lasts"* with glowing button
- **Footer**: Address (3A Adeyemi Lawson Street, Ikoyi, Lagos), phone (+234 901 202 0202), email ([info@kodaiconstruction.com](mailto:info@kodaiconstruction.com)), social links, and quick navigation

### 3. Services Page

- Hero with headline *"Complete Construction Delivery, From Concept to Concrete"*
- 4 detailed service sections with scroll-triggered fade-in animations:
  1. **Integrated Design & Build** — Architectural, Structural, MEP Design
  2. **General Construction Contracting** — Site development, high-rise builds, finishing
  3. **Construction & Technical Consulting** — Feasibility, BOQ reviews, audits
  4. **Engineering-Grade Coordination** — MEP integration, smart systems, energy efficiency
- Each section expands on hover/click with detailed bullet points

## Interactive & Visual Effects Throughout

- **Loading screen** logo animation on initial visit
- **Scroll-triggered animations**: sections fade/slide in as you scroll
- **Button glow effects**: Navy-to-gold gradient glow on hover
- **Image hover zoom**: Portfolio images scale up subtly on hover
- **Parallax backgrounds**: Hero sections with subtle parallax depth
- **Animated number counters**: For stats like years of experience, projects completed
- **Smooth page transitions**: Fade between route changes
- **Mobile responsive**: Hamburger menu, stacked layouts, touch-friendly interactions
